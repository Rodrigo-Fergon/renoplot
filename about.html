About RenoPlot

Free, accurate calculators to help you plan home improvement projects with confidence.

Why RenoPlot exists

Most material calculators online are either too generic, buried under ads, or built for a single market. RenoPlot was created to fix that: a single place with clear, accurate calculators that work for both US and UK measurements, built around how people actually plan a project, not just a raw formula.

What we offer

Every calculator on RenoPlot is built around a real project scenario, not just a single number. We account for the details that change the result in practice, such as waste factor, openings, pattern matching, and unit conversions between feet, meters, and metric equivalents.

  • Paint, flooring, tile, and drywall calculators for interior projects
  • Concrete, gravel, and fencing calculators for outdoor and structural projects
  • Mulch and wallpaper calculators with project-specific adjustments
  • Every calculator supports both US and UK units natively

How estimates are calculated

Each calculator uses standard industry formulas for material coverage, combined with a configurable waste factor to account for cuts, mistakes, and pattern matching. Results are meant to give you a confident starting point for your shopping list, not a replacement for a professional measurement on complex or safety-critical projects.

What RenoPlot does not do

RenoPlot does not provide calculators involving structural, electrical, or plumbing safety calculations. Our focus is strictly on material quantity estimation for general home improvement projects.
