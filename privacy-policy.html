Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RenoPlot ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit renoplot.com. We built RenoPlot to be simple and respectful of your privacy, and this page explains exactly what data is involved.

Information we collect

RenoPlot's calculators run entirely in your browser. The numbers you enter into a calculator (such as room dimensions or project measurements) are never sent to our servers, stored, or shared. They exist only on your device while you use the tool.

We do collect limited, non-identifying usage data through Google Analytics, described below.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use RenoPlot, such as which calculators are most popular and which pages need improvement. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect data such as your approximate location, device type, browser, and pages visited. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising and Cookies

RenoPlot may display advertisements served by Google AdSense and other third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites, in order to provide advertisements about goods and services that may interest you.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to RenoPlot and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendor cookies through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

Children's privacy

RenoPlot is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site function, such as ad personalization, but will not affect the core functionality of any calculator.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Questions about this policy? Visit our Contact page.
